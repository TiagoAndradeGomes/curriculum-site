About Me

I am a Civil Engineer with an Integrated Master’s degree from Instituto Superior Técnico and member no. 77914 of the Portuguese Order of Engineers. I currently serve as BIM Manager on the largest contract awarded by Lisbon City Council: the General Drainage Plan, focused on building the urban drainage tunnels and associated works.

I continually pursue professional excellence through specialised and complementary training, holding postgraduate diplomas in Management (ISEG) and BIM Coordination (ISEP), in addition to the Senior Occupational Safety‑Technician course and Train‑the‑Trainer certification. I have solid experience in public procurement, BIM methodologies, geotechnical infrastructure and hydraulics.

I practise judo and hold a 1st-dan black belt, an experience that greatly strengthens my discipline, resilience and teamwork skills.

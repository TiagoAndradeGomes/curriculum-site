About Me

Civil Engineer (Integrated Master's Degree from Instituto Superior Técnico), BIM Manager with over 8 years of experience managing and executing large-scale hydraulic infrastructure and complex underground projects. Currently, I lead the implementation of BIM methodologies for the Lisbon Drainage Master Plan (PGDL), a strategic project valued at €132 million, with a particular emphasis on integrating BIM with Geographic Information Systems (GIS), multidisciplinary technical coordination, and information management. My core training in civil engineering is complemented by specializations in BIM Coordination (ISEP) and Management (ISEG), supported by solid expertise in public procurement, occupational safety, and training. I am an active member of various technical associations, notably serving on the Board of Directors of buildingSMART Portugal and coordinating the BIM working group of the Portuguese Tunnelling and Underground Space Commission (CPT). These roles involve initiatives promoting openBIM standardization and activities within the tunneling and underground construction sector.
